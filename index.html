Bringing Inclusiveness to Tech

#FSTOCONF15

Full Stack Toronto brings web development to the city, focusing on intermediate and senior developers. And like any industry that concentrates on growing, connecting the community is key.

Enter #FSTOCONF15, an annual conference that combines speakers, industry pros, developers and anyone interested in taking their skills to the next level.

This Year's Theme: Inclusivity in the Tech Industry

One of the downsides of the tech industry is it's heavily skewed towards a very narrow demographic, which isn't necessarily the best way of drawing in the best and brightest minds.
